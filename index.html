Create Account

Sign up for a parent portal account to begin the enrollment process.

Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need?

Birth certificate, proof of residence, previous records, and immunization records.

Can I choose any school?

Schools are assigned based on residence, but transfer options are available.

When can I enroll?

Enrollment is open year-round. Priority for the upcoming year begins in January.
