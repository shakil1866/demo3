FAQ

Common Questions

Find answers to frequently asked questions about enrollment.

When can I enroll my child?

Enrollment is open year-round. For the upcoming school year, we recommend enrolling by July 1st to ensure your child is assigned to their preferred school.

How do I know which school my child will attend?

Students are assigned to schools based on their home address. Use our School Finder tool to determine which school serves your area.

Can I transfer my child to a different school?

In-district transfers are available based on capacity. Submit a transfer request form for consideration. Out-of-district requests require superintendent approval.

What if I don't have all the required documents?

You can start the enrollment process and upload documents later. However, final enrollment cannot be completed until all required documents are submitted.
