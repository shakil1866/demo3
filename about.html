Our History

Milestones

A journey of growth and excellence since our founding.

1952

District Founded

Excellence School District was established with just 3 schools and 500 students, serving our growing community with a vision for educational excellence.

1978

High School Opens

Our flagship high school opened its doors, expanding our offerings to serve students through grade 12.

1995

Technology Initiative

Launched our pioneering technology integration program, becoming one of the first districts in the state to provide computers to all classrooms.

2010

National Recognition

Received national recognition for academic achievement and was named a Model District for educational excellence.

2024

Expansion Complete

Expanded to 24 schools serving over 15,000 students, with state-of-the-art facilities and innovative programs.
